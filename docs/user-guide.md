Welcome to Tabletop Mastering, the platform to manage your games and tabletop gaming groups. This guide will help you use the website easily, without needing technical knowledge.

## 1. Accessing the Website
- Go to https://tabletopmastering.games from your computer, mobile, or tablet.
- You don't need to install anything. Just open your browser and access it.

## 2. User Registration
- Click "Sign Up" on the home page.
- Fill in your name, email, and password.
- Click "Create Account". You'll receive a confirmation email.

## 3. Logging In
- Click "Log In".
- Enter your email and password.
- Click "Enter" to access your dashboard.

## 4. Create or Join a Group
- Go to the "Groups" menu.
- To create a new one, click "Create Group", give it a name and description.
- To join an existing group, search for it and click "Join".

## 5. Adding Games
- Access the "Games" menu.
- Click "Add Game" and search for the title (you can use BoardGameGeek).
- Fill in the details and save.

## 6. Recording a Match
- Go to "Matches" and click "Record Match".
- Choose the game, group, and participants.
- Add the results and comments if you want.
- Click "Save".

## 7. View History and Statistics
- In "History" you'll see all your matches and results.
- In "Rankings" you can see player and group standings.
- Use filters to search by date, game, or group.

## 8. Settings and Privacy
- Access your profile to change personal data or password.
- You can configure notifications and privacy from the "Settings" menu.

## 9. Help and Support
- If you have questions, use the "Help" button or contact the team via the contact form.

---

**Ready! You can now enjoy and manage your games easily.**

If you have any problems, ask your group or contact support. The website is designed to be intuitive and accessible for everyone.
